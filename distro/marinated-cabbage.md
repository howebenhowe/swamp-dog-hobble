## marinated cabbage
yield: 5000g

### ratios
| ingredient    | quantity |
|:--------------|---------:|
| cabbage      |    5000g |
| salt          |     1.3% |
| spice mix     |     1.3% |
| ginger/garlic |     1.3% |

### method {#marinated-cabbage-method}
1. cut cabbages to quarters through core
1. remove core
1. cut to 1.5" pieces
1. toss all ingredients together in large cambro
1. marinade for 3 hours
