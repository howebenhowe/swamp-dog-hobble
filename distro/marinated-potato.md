## marinated potato
yield 10 qts

### ratios
| ingredient    | quantity |
|:--------------|---------:|
| potatoes      |    5000g |
| salt          |     1.3% |
| spice mix     |     1.3% |
| ginger/garlic |     1.3% |
| canola        |     1/3c |

### method {#potato-method}
1. peel potatoes
1. cut to loose dice, \~1" cubes
1. mix all ingredients
1. store in fridge overnight
